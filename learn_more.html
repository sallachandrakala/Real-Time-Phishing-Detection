How It Works

From URL submission to final verdict, our engine performs a comprehensive 3-stage audit.

1. Structural Analysis

We parse the URL to identify obfuscation techniques, IP encoding, and typosquatting attempts (e.g., 'g0ogle.com') that mimic legitimate brands.

2. Forensic Data Mining

Our system queries global WHOIS databases, DNS records, and SSL certificate authorities to verify the domain's age, issuer, and ownership history.

3. Risk Scoring

Using a weighted algorithm, we calculate a Threat Score (0-100) and generate a detailed report classifying the site as Safe, Suspicious, or Phishing.
